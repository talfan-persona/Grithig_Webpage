Sightseeing

Local Sightseeing & Attractions

The area around Y Grithig is rich in natural beauty, history, and culture. From ancient castles to stunning waterfalls, there's something to captivate every visitor.

Natural Attractions

Waterfalls

  • Henrhyd Falls: The tallest waterfall in South Wales (15 minutes drive)
  • Sgwd yr Eira: The famous "waterfall you can walk behind" (25 minutes drive)
  • Four Waterfalls Walk: A spectacular circular walk featuring four different waterfalls (20 minutes drive)

National Parks & Reserves

  • Brecon Beacons National Park: World-class hiking and stunning landscapes (30 minutes drive)
  • Fforest Fawr: Ancient landscapes and dark sky reserve perfect for stargazing
  • Craig y Nos Country Park: Beautiful parkland with easy walking trails (10 minutes drive)

Historical Sites

Castles & Heritage

  • Carreg Cennen Castle: Dramatic clifftop castle with breathtaking views (45 minutes drive)
  • Dinefwr Castle: Historic castle set in beautiful parkland (35 minutes drive)
  • National Showcaves Centre: Underground adventure and dinosaur park (20 minutes drive)

Museums & Culture

  • National Botanic Garden of Wales: World-famous garden with the largest single-span glasshouse (40 minutes drive)
  • Dylan Thomas Centre: Celebrating Wales' most famous poet (1 hour drive to Swansea)
